Jollof Rice

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Canned & Fresh Tomato
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chicken stock
  • Seasoning and Herbs (curry poweder, salt, black/white pepper, bouillon powder, Thyme, and Bay leaves)

Preparation Steps:

Step 1: Sauteeing the Onion

Step 2: Add the blended peppers

Step 3: Add the Thyme, curry powder, salt, white pepper (good but optional), and seasoning cubes

Step 4: Stir in the Rice

Step 5: Add the chicken stock

Step 6: Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook until the rice is done

Step 7: Serve your food and enjoy

Tips and Variations

Tip 1: After Stirring in the Rice, cover the pot with a tight lid. This needs to be done because Jollof Rice needs a lot of steam to turn out right

Tip 2: The amount of time your blended peppers need to be heated before you add seasoning depends on how much water was in it before it was put on the fire. The more the initial water in it, the longer it has to stay on the fire before seasoning can be added

Variation: The Chicken stock can be replaced with either a Beef or Vegetable stock
